Students

So much to offer

Our automated features

Internships 

Jobaskit offers several internships for the students where recruiters can post unlimited internships for each kind of profile.

Jobs 

Students can apply for jobs while recruiters can find the best applicants with the help of jobaskit’s recommended profiles with accurate data.

Company 

Companies can hire the best interns with the help of recommended profiles and update their career page with the help of jobaskit.

Campus 

A campus can link with jobaskit to get placement in top-recruited companies connected to jobaskit and make the placement process easy.

Entrepreneur 

Entrepreneurs have the chance of building a rich employer branding with the help of jobaskit’s unique features.

Quizz 

Quizzes held by jobaskit improves students’ skill and provide data to recruiters helping them in finding the best interns for their companies.

Freelance 

People can easily hire freelancers describing their project details, and freelancers have the opportunity to be a part of these projects.
